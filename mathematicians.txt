Euclid: 
  - Greek Mathematician that lived Around 300 BC.
  - Lived in Alexandria (Which was post-Macedonian at the time).
  Contributions
    ◻ Euclidean Algorithm.
    ◻ Infinitude of Prime Numbers.
    ◻ Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic.
    ◻ Euclidean Geometry.
  Ouvre
    ◻ Elements. a 13-book series compiling the entirety of Greek mathematical knowledge at the time.
